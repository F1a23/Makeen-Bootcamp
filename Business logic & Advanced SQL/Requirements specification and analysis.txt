Requirement Specification

A university wants to manage data about students, courses, and enrollments.
Each student has a unique student ID, full name, email, phone number, and date of birth.
Each course has a course code, title, credit hours, and department.
A student can enroll in many courses, and each course can have many students.
When a student enrolls in a course, the university records the semester, academic year, and the grade obtained.
Professors teach courses, and each professor has a unique ID, name, office number, and specialization.
Each course is taught by one professor, but a professor can teach multiple courses.


üè• Exercise 2: Hospital Management System
Requirement Specification

A hospital keeps track of patients, doctors, and appointments.
Each doctor has a unique doctor ID, name, specialty, phone number, and years of experience.
Each patient has a patient ID, name, gender, date of birth, address, and emergency contact.
Appointments connect patients and doctors. Each appointment has an appointment ID, date, time, and reason for visit.
A patient can have multiple appointments with different doctors, and each doctor can see many patients.
The hospital also records prescriptions linked to appointments, containing medicine name, dosage, and duration.

Requirement Specification

Each room has a room number, type (single, double, suite), status (available or occupied), and price per n ight.
